Session Norms

Getting to Yes — Principles

01Separate people from the problem

Address the issue, not the person. Be soft on people, hard on the problem.

02Focus on interests, not positions

Ask 'why?' to uncover the underlying needs behind stated positions.

03Invent options for mutual gain

Brainstorm multiple solutions before deciding. Separate invention from decision.

04Insist on objective criteria

Use fair standards to evaluate options, not pressure or power.

Mediator Tip

The mediator keeps the session aligned with these principles. Guide the group to separate people from the problem, uncover interests, and defer judgment during brainstorming.
